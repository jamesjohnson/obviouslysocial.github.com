Team

Another great thing about us? In addition to being social media experts, we're smart and fun to work with. Meet the team!

MAE KARWOWSKI, Founder & CEO / @maewow

Mae is the owner of Obviously Social. Widely recognized for her thought leadership in social, she directs the social media strategy, web, app development and community management for clients across all industries, including e-commerce and education. Before founding Obviously Social, Mae directed social media for Gilt City, part of Gilt Groupe, and oversaw social execution for clients such as Bravo, JCPenney, Coca Cola, UGG and Alamo Rent-A-Car with the agency 360i. She attended St Paul’s School and then matriculated at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with honors and a B.A. in Philosophy.

LAUREN B. MANGIAFORTE, Strategist / @LBMForte

Lauren is a writer and communications expert. (A Creative Agency). Hailing from Chicagoland, Lauren is a graduate of Saint Mary’s College (Notre Dame, IN) and holds a master’s degree in literature from the University of St Andrews in Scotland. Sandia Trent, VP, Strategy & BD

Sandia Trent is an digital marketing leader and analyst. Before joining Obviously Social, she led data and content-driven marketing efforts at media giants such as Vimeo and Inc. 500 startups. Chuck Routhier, Designer

Chuck has over 25 years of design experience. He has worked on comprehensive branding programs for Hertz, Blackberry, Burger King, Capital One, Clairol, Exubera, Lipitor, Maxwell House, Merrill Lynch, Morgan Stanley, PowerStop/American Express, Cetaphil, Song Airlines and Thomas’s. Chuck was creative director at Landor Associates in NYC, then Vice President/Creative Director of Brand Anatomy, the internal branding studio of CDM. Prior to Landor, Chuck was associate Creative Director at CKS Partners in New York and Art Director at Clement Mok designs in San Francisco where he worked with Apple and other technology companies in the dawn of the digital design era. In addition, he has been a principal owner of several small design firms in New York and San Francisco. He co-founded dsgnfix, a search, discover and inspiration iPhone app (dsgnfix.com). Chuck holds an MFA in Graphic Design from Yale University and has taught design at Yale, Maine College of Art and Parsons School of Design in Manhattan. Chuck also designs and crafts lighting and furniture. He has two sons and a daughter.
