We had the best time wine tasting in Napa, and wanted to suggest some of our favorite spots. The good news though is that you can't go wrong, we had fun everywhere we visited.

  • Ma(i)sonry* - Amazing wine, not a winery but a collection of the area. Our Favorite!
  • Domaine Chandon - in Yountville, stop here on the drive in for bubbles!
  • Hall Winery in St. Helena - Cool building, great wines, a fun place to visit.
  • Beringer in St. Helena - A classic and more "touristy", but a fun place to stop.
  • Robert Sinskey in Napa Great wine, beautiful vineyard and a fun place to stop. Feed the fish.

Here are also a few links to check out that have more information:

  • Napa Valley Wineries
  • Visit Napa
  • Napa Tourist Guide

The only thing possibly better than wine tasting in Napa is the food. WOW. We can't fit all the amazing spots on this page, but we included some of the highlights. They aren't all necessarily "affordable", but they are worth it!

  • Redd - We went to dinner here, outdoor fireplace, in Yountville.
  • Auberge du Soleil - Fancy but incredible. Great view, even better food.
  • Bouchon - More afforbale Thomas Keller option, great for lunch.
  • JoLē - Dining in Calistoga, haven't been but heard great things.
  • Solbar - Amazing food, fun atomsphere, we dined outside.
  • Oakville Grocer - Sandwiches to go, really fun grocery.
  • Tra Vigne - Welcome party is here, but great anytime of day.
  • Model Bakery - Bomb.com breakfast.

And if you're feeling super fancy...

  • French Laudry - Truffles anyone?

Napa has beautiful wedding most of the year, but especially in May (fingers crossed!). There should be some good opportunities to hit the outdoors, and we thought of a few things you could do during your stay:

  • Yoga - The bride-to-be will be getting her OM on.
  • Golf - We haven't played there, but imagine its beautiful.
  • Hiking - Mt. St. Helena is the highest peak in the San Francisco watershed and the hike is five miles long to the summit
  • Picnics - Swing by the oakville grocer, grab some food and head to V. Sattui!
  • Uber is everywhere in Napa and its defintiely the easiest way to get around.
  • Silvercar is an amazing car rental option. We just did it in Dallas and it was cheaper than any other service, and cool Audi A4. Highly recommended
  • Black Tie Tax is a Taxi service, the number is (707) 259-1000.
  • Napa Wine Tour We used Rod to take us around Napa for the day for some wine tasting, and it was great!
